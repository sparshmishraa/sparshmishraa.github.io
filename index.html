Adidas US Sales Analysis

This project analyzed sales data to identify key trends and insights. New York and Florida emerged as top-performing states, while 'In-store' sales dominated as the primary sales method. Monthly trends revealed seasonal spikes, with 2021 significantly outperforming 2020 in total sales. Regionally, the West and Northeast accounted for half of all sales, with Men's Athletic Footwear driving the highest profits.

Health Stats of LVH Patients

This project explored heart disease trends, revealing that 85.6% of cases occur in the elderly, with minimal prevalence in younger groups. Cholesterol levels are notably higher in females than males, and asymptomatic cases dominate pain type distribution. Key correlations were identified: blood pressure rises with age, while maximum heart rate declines, offering deeper insights into age-related cardiovascular health patterns.
